True Colors

Personal color analysis is the practice of identifying the colors most suited to an individual, based on their eye color, hair color, skin tone, and overall contrast in their coloring. This project applied various user-centered design methods to research and prototype an Android app that analyzes users’ personal coloring within seconds, offers access to related insights and enables the purchase of clothes in suitable colors.

The case study is under construction. In the meantime, feel free to try out the prototype.
